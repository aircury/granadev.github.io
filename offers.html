Join our team and help us build quality software solutions

Software Developer Position

We are looking for talented developers to join our team. This is an excellent opportunity for those starting their career in software development.

What are we looking for?

We are looking for a junior programmer who has a passion for web application development. For this position we do not require previous experience. You need to know how to program.

We are looking for someone who is good at programming, eager to learn and grow. We don't need you to have formal education, but you will have to demonstrate that you have aptitudes for this position.

Have sufficient English level to be able to communicate well with native speakers. You don't need to have formal education or any certificate, but to give you an idea, around B2 level. Several of the colleagues are English and don't speak Spanish.

What would make you stand out?

For this position, it is essential to have a disability certificate equal to or greater than 33%.

We're not looking for all of this, it's a list of qualities that if you have any, would make you stand out:

  • Demonstrating your creativity, originality and willingness to work.
  • Being up to date with industry trends.
  • Having experience with some version control system, ideally Git.
  • Currently studying or having completed a vocational training cycle or degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Alternatively, demonstrating that you have had an interest in learning things independently, such as taking online courses.
  • If you have some experience with any of the technologies we use, it always helps (PHP, Javascript, Symfony, Laravel, Python, .NET, Ruby, Postgres, AWS, React, Vue).

What will the selection process be like?

  • We review your profile and see if it could fit with what we're looking for or not.
  • First interview and initial contact.
  • English level test.
  • Code test.
  • In-depth interview.

If you need any adaptation in the tests or interviews, please let us know so we can make the necessary adjustments.

What do we offer?

  • For this position we are looking for someone at point T1 to D4 on our scale (Intern to Junior), which means from 16,576 to 23,042 euros gross per year.
  • Permanent contract.
  • On-site work mode.
  • Full-time schedule.
  • Annual contract review.
  • Great opportunities for progression.
  • Fantastic access to professional development resources.
  • Budget for training, conferences, etc.
  • Flexible hours within the 7:30-19:30h interval, to overlap with colleagues.
  • Great co-workers who are very happy to work at this company.
  • Optional workshops and activities to socialize (health, cooking, board games...)

If you are interested in this offer, please send your CV to:

info@granadev.es
